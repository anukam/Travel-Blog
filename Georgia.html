Georgia

Georgia is one of the oldest countries in the world. It has its own language, alphabet, cuisine, national folclor, dances and many more traditions. Georgia is officially the first wine making country in the world, and thus wines they have to offer are really good. You can visit Georgia anytime during the year as it offers sea for summer and ski-resorts during winter. Unfortunately, 20% of of Georgia is occupied by Russia, but Georgia keeps developing as a country and is definitely one of the top destinations in Europe.
