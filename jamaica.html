Jamaica

Jamaica is an island which is located in the Caribbean. This island gem is sorrounded by the cool Caribbean Sea. The island has over the years become a number one holiday destination because of its vibrant culture and its breath-taking natural landscape. National Motto: Out of Many One People it signifies the diversity of people on the island .
